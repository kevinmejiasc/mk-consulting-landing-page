The Real Cost

01 / Too slow

They leave before it loads

Visitors abandon a site that takes more than a few seconds to open — on the exact click you paid for with an ad or earned on Google. A slow page hands your customer to whoever loads first.

02 / Broken on mobile

Broken where it counts — the phone

Most of your visitors are on a phone, and Google now ranks your site on its mobile version. A site that pinches, overflows, or buries the "call" button loses the customer right there.

03 / No next step

Nothing tells them to act

Most small-business sites describe the company and stop — no booking, no form up top, no reason to act now. Traffic arrives, reads, and leaves, and you never even know they were there.

Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

How much does a custom website cost? +

It depends on what you need — a focused lead-gen site is very different from an e-commerce store or a custom web app. We scope to the problem and give you a transparent, itemized estimate before you commit. Start with a free evaluation and you get a real number, not a mystery.

How long does it take to build a website? +

Most marketing and lead-gen sites launch in a few weeks, not months, because we run a defined process with clear milestones. After your evaluation we give you a firm timeline and hit it. The most common delay is waiting on content, so we can write the copy and source images to keep things moving.

Do I own my website and everything on it? +

Yes — completely. The code, the content, the domain, and all the accounts are yours. No proprietary platform, no lock-in, and no situation where you can only make changes by paying us forever.

Will my new site actually show up on Google? Is SEO included? +

We build every site the way Google rewards — fast pages, clean technical SEO, mobile-first structure, and proper local signals — plus clear calls to action so the traffic converts. No honest team can promise a specific ranking, but we build the foundation that earns visibility and captures the leads you get. New sites typically take a few months to gain search traction.

Do you build on WordPress or custom-code it? Which is right for me? +

Whichever actually serves your goals — we don't force one stack on every client. For many businesses a lean, fast custom build outperforms a plugin-heavy site; for others, an easy-to-edit CMS is the right call. We recommend the option that's fastest, most secure, and easiest for you to live with, and we explain why.

Will the site be mobile-friendly and fast? +

Yes — and we design for the phone first. Most of your visitors are on mobile, and Google ranks your site based on its mobile version, so the mobile experience isn't an afterthought. Fast load times are built in, because speed affects both rankings and whether a visitor stays.

Who writes the copy and provides the images? +

We can. We write conversion-focused copy and source or create the visuals, so you're not stuck staring at a blank page. If you have brand assets, photos, or copy you love, we build around them. If you don't, we handle it.

What happens after launch — hosting, updates, maintenance? Do you work outside Atlanta? +

Your call: we hand off a site you can manage yourself, and we offer ongoing hosting, updates, and support if you'd rather we handle it — with a real Atlanta team you can reach, not a freelancer who disappears. We're based in Atlanta, GA and build for businesses across the United States remotely.
