Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

What's included in the online presence service? +

Google Business Profile optimization, local near-me SEO, a reviews and reputation system, consistent business info across every major listing, AI-search (AEO/GEO) visibility, and proper setup of your core social profiles. Done for you, then maintained.

How long until I see results? +

Foundational wins — an optimized profile, consistent listings, a working review flow — show up within a few weeks. Ranking and AI-recommendation momentum builds over roughly 2 to 4 months as the signals compound. Anyone promising a 30-day #1 ranking is a red flag; we tell you upfront what's fast and what takes time.

Is AI search optimization (AEO/GEO) real, or hype? +

Real, and measurable. Customers increasingly ask ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google's AI Overviews for recommendations, and those tools return a shortlist of only a few names. AEO/GEO is the work of structuring your info, listings, reviews, and site so they can find and recommend you. As an AI studio, it's the part we do best.

Do you handle the reviews and social profiles too? +

Yes. We build and run the systems. For reviews, we set up the request flow and respond on your behalf with your approval. For social, we set your profiles up right and on-brand. You stay focused on serving customers.

Do I own my Google profile, listings, and accounts? +

Yes — always. Your Google Business Profile, listings, social accounts, and website stay in your name and control. We optimize what's yours; we never hold it hostage.

Is this a monthly service or a one-time project? +

Both options exist. Some clients start with a one-time setup and cleanup; most keep a light monthly engagement, because reviews, listings, rankings, and AI signals need ongoing upkeep to hold. We recommend the right fit at your audit and report on calls and bookings, not vanity metrics.

Will this work for my industry? +

If customers search for what you do — locally or by category — yes. It's strongest for service businesses, trades, professional services, retail, and local practices where near-me search and reputation drive the decision. We'll tell you honestly if it's not a fit.

Are you Atlanta-based? Do you work with businesses elsewhere? +

We're in Atlanta and know the local market firsthand, and we serve clients across the United States remotely. Presence work travels well — the audit is the same wherever you are.
