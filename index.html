Frequently Asked

Questions, answered.

What does Gamma Tech Group actually do? +

We map your operational workflows, identify the friction points costing you time and money, and deploy a tailored mix of AI agents, software integrations, and automation to eliminate them — so your team focuses on revenue, not repetition.

Who is the right fit for your services? +

Businesses generating $250K+ in annual revenue that are bottlenecked by manual processes, disconnected software, or admin work that's distracting from growth. We work across industries — accounting, real estate, legal, e-commerce, professional services, and more.

How long until I see results? +

Most clients see measurable time savings within the first 30 days of deployment. Full ROI on automation builds typically materializes within 60–90 days, depending on scope.

Do I need technical knowledge to work with you? +

None. We handle the entire technical lift — from audit to architecture to deployment to ongoing maintenance. Your job is to know your business; ours is to translate that into systems.

Do you build mobile and web apps? +

Yes. Alongside AI automation and integrations, we design and build custom mobile and web applications — customer-facing products or internal tools — with AI and automation engineered in from the start.

What does engagement typically cost? +

Engagements are scoped to the problem. Most projects start with a paid discovery and audit phase, followed by build sprints priced by deliverable. We'll give you a transparent estimate after your evaluation — no mystery pricing.

Are you Atlanta-based? Do you work with clients elsewhere? +

Yes — we're based in Atlanta, GA, and we serve clients across the U.S. remotely. For Atlanta-area businesses, on-site discovery sessions are available on request.
